Privacy Policy

Data Collection

NO data is collected at all. Since no data is collected, it stands to reason, and is, indeed, the case, that none of your data can be stored at or sold to third parties.

That being said, there are a few other ways your data is used.

Chrome Storage API

The application take advantage of Google Chrome's Storage API to locally state information. This is similar to a cookie. Most of the use cases are to save your personal settings of the application. Google syncs this information across your various computers and Chrome installations. However, the application only access this data locally on your machine and they DO NOT transmit it to any third-party.
