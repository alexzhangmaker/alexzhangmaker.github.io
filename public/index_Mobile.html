You’ll notice that the first class added to every innermost div is “col-12". This is to specify that when the screen size is extra small (min-width of 576px or less), the column will occupy the entire row (all 12 columns).

Specifying breakpoints in the columns with “col-md-[number of columns]”, will adjust the way the elements will be rendered on medium (and above) screen sizes. Adding “order-md-last” or “order-md-first” will control the visual order of the content starting from the medium breakpoint.
